Field of view overlay demo

How to use

  • Click the circle icon to open the dialog.
  • Set a diameter (mm/µm), then click Place field of view.
  • Move the mouse to preview; click to drop circles (repeat as needed).
  • This page runs a separate annotation toolkit overlay (default + ruler) on the same slide. Use Ruler to draw a measurement and compare visually to a dropped FOV ring (two Paper layers; they do not share geometry).
  • If clicks do not reach the ruler, press Ruler or Default again—the toolkit overlay is brought forward. Use the circle / binoculars control for FOV; that overlay brings itself forward when activated.
